00 Background

Within skeletal muscle, there is a certain receptor that are part of cells called platelet-derived growth factor receptor (PDGFR-alpha). These receptors are capable of regulating cell growth and proliferation in multiple tissues. They are able to mark a certain cell, called fibro-adipogenic progenitors (FAP), that is capable of differentiating into a fat cell. FAP cells are also underlie muscular dystrophy and chronic kidney disease.

01 Problem Statement

Is it possible for FAP remodeling in skeletal muscle to induce weight loss in obese patients?

02 Preliminary Data

We collected a series of data in order to form our hypothesis that weight loss can be induced through certain remodeling of fibro-adipogenic receptors.
